SOCIETY

Society in mid 21st century

Society has changed a lot since 2019. The development of AI during corona virus crisis has been responsible for various reforms in society.

  • Education-Schools do not exist anymore. Students are taught online. All teaching process is done with help of AI.

  • Culture-There are no more social gatherings, ceremonies, festivals. People have become so indulged and dependent on AI that they have forgot to interact with each other. AI is controlling their minds.

  • Religion-The religious beliefs of people had come to an end. They have lost faith in religion.

  • Government started taking help of AI in governing the country. They became essential part of government.

Society in 2100

As the population has reduced people live in small groups. AI has become so powerful that in few parts of the world these small groups are ruled by robots. Tough situations have made people surrender to them. They have to serve robots. Weak people are exterminated as they are of no use to robots.To survive in this new world one has to be fit. AI has developed a vast network through which they keep a track of all the activities of a person. Infact no activity of a person can be kept hidden from them. AI has began to conquer the world.
Now there are no more conflicts based on religion, caste or creed. People have realized their mistake of letting the AI take control over them. People from all across the world have united as one to fight against AI. They need your help, guardians.
