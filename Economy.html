ECONOMY

Unemployment

Economy has also been affected the most. Millions of people are facing unemployment. There has significant transformation in business, education, market and government sector due to AI.

  • With enhanced automation and machine learning vehicles were designed that are capable of sensing environment and move safely with no or little human input. These vehicles are self-driven and do not require any human driver for its movement. With the growth in these automated vehicles the demand for professionals like car drivers, sailors and pilot would rapidly dropdown.
  • Electronic-Commerce has undergone massive transformation due to AI. Robots navigate the space to collect products and execute customer orders. They deliver orders to customers with the help of autonomous drones and cars. This reduced the demand for salespersons and network stores.
  • There would be no need for a nurse to monitor patients health at regular intervals as patients are being monitored by robots.

AI Took Over

Like most of the sectors had been taken over by AI economy too was in hands of AI. Old currencies were no more in use. Only one currency, ie, cryptocurrency was prevalent all over the world. Only digital transactions could be made. This currency was decided by AI. People had to depend on AI in order for food and survival. They controlled the market and business. All the major decisions of the company were made by them.
