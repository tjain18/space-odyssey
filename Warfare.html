WARFARE STRATEGIES

AI's Side

  • Robots have smart weapons which are very strong and powerful. They can destroy entire human race with it.
  • They have a vast connected network all across the world.
  • They track human activities.
  • If they find anything suspicious they exterminate the person immediately.
  • Many people had to surrender to them and work under them.




Our Side

Robots are unaware of the guardians. Only they can help us win the war against AI. Art of designing or programming is not left among new generation. They became totally dependent on AI for process of production of machines and algorithms after the computer-integrated manufacturing allowed computers to control the entire production process. We cannot destroy the network of these AI until we create our own network. However this new generation does not know how to create their own network without getting caught by AI.
This art of designing or producing machines is their in guardians. They need to secretly develop their own network without getting caught by AI else they will be executed by robots. For this to happen they need fake identities in order to buy essentials for living while work secretly to build their own network. Fake identity will be provided to you shortly by people. They will help you hide your identities. If you need any help feel free to approach people around you.
Smart weapons developed using AI can be lethal for society and can lead to global arms race to another level. We need to stop them from manufacturing more weapons to save our human race. If we manage to hack into their systems and take control of the smart weapon we will be able to save our world and make it a better place again.
