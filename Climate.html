CLIMATE

Global Warming

Global warming is defined as the gradual increase in the overall temperature of the Earth's atmosphere generally attributed to the greenhouse effect caused by increased levels of carbon dioxide, CFCs and other pollutants. The main reasons for global warming were:

  • Burning fossil fuels for production of electricity, petroleum caused emission of carbon dioxide in atmosphere.
  • Deforestation- Due to this CO2 released in atmosphere was not absorbed by plants.
  • Agriculture and farming- livestock like sheep and cattle, produce methane, a greenhouse gas

However as most of the human population was wiped out by 2025, so the main reasons for global warming were not these anymore. Temperature started to come back to normal as global warming reduced.

AI Controlled Cimate

AI became so powerful that they could control climate. AI enabled spacecrafts reached the asteroid belts, while on Earth the technology tame climate change. AI had built massive swarms of drones to reflect sunlight away from the oceans. As a result temperature of ocean reduced drastically hereby affecting climate all over the world. Green land submerged and it was beginning of pre ice-age era. Even countries like India was experiencing sub-zero temperatures. It was freezing in almost all parts of the world.




How this climate benefits AI

The cold weather helped AI by keeping there processors cool. This increased their life. As we had entered into pre ice-age era there was no more agriculture possible. So there was shortage of food for people. Moreover humans cannot survive in such a cold weather. So many people died due to starvation and cold. They had to depend on robots for heat and food. This helped robots to takeover humans and rule them. So this climate helped robots in almost all aspects.
