HISTORY

Brief

After covid 19 was reported the world seemed to change forever. In few years most of the population was wiped off due to this deadly virus and only 20-25% of them survived. People started getting more dependent on AI. There was a great machine uprising. Each and everything even our household machines had been taken over by AI. They had replaced humans in almost all the fields. Gradually artificial intelligence (AI) started to become the dominant form of intelligence on Earth, with computer programs or robots effectively taking the control of the planet away from the human species. There was unemployment which lead to economic crisis which lead to conflicts among countries. Computer-integrated manufacturing allowed computers to control the entire production process. AI was able to modify its own source code and increase its own intelligence. AI started to conquer the world. In many parts of the world human had to surrender to them. They had built a strong network connection all across the world. Hiding anything from them was getting almost impossible. They could track down all the activities of a person. Art of developing machines was lost by this time. However guardians who went for hibernation in 2020 possessed this art. Only they can help us win the war against AI by destroying their network.

Timeline


  • 2019

    • First case of Covid-19 was reported in Wuhan, China in 2019. In few months this deadly virus spread among lakhs of people all across the world.

  • 2020

    • Over 100 countries worldwide had instituted either a full or partial lockdown by the end of March 2020, affecting billions of people. During this time polymath people were sent into hibernation as the future of humanity appeared darker day by day.

  • 2025

    • Many deadly variants of corona virus had been reported wiping out most of the world human population. Only 20-25% managed to survive. Those who survived were facing post-covid symptoms. People started using AI to fulfil their day to day requirement.

  • 2040

    • As there was lack of capable people there was economy crisis in many parts of the world. US was affected the most. There was a situation of war all across the globe.

  • 2050

    • People started believing that country that lead in teachnologies using artificial intelligence will dominate the globe. Countries indulged in making their artificial intelligence more powerful.

  • 2060

    • World war 3 broke out between China and United States. Many countries were involved in this war. Robots were used in warfare. This resulted in deaths of thousands of people and robots emerged as heroes.

  • 2070

    • World became completely automated. AI were used to beat stock markets, diagnose heart disease, to serve customers online and almost in every field

  • 2075

    • Algorithms free from human programmers are training themselves on massive data sets. Ultimate goal was to make a self teaching discipline that can out perform human across a wide range of disciplines. They started programming themselves and had grown more powerful breaking all the robotics law.

  • 2085

    • AI began taking millions of jobs away from people like drivers, radiologists, insurance adjusters etc. This made government to pay unemployed citizen a universal basic income freeing them to pursue their dream unburdened by the need to earn a living.

  • 2100

    • AI took over all sorts of machine all over the world. Even house hold items were controlled by them. In some places they became so dominant that people had to surrender to them.
